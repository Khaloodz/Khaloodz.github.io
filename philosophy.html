  • My Laptop runs a GNU/ Linux distribution called Arch Linux, with suckless' dwm, and only FOSS Applications.

Why GNU/ Linux?

  • I am not into proprietary shit, and I do not like someone else to decide what is best for me to have on my fucking personal computer. The only options for me were BSD/ *NIX solutions, and I decided to go with Linux.

Why Arch Linux?

  • While many people moved to Artix or Void due to SystemD concerns, I personally do not mind systemd's shit. They know their shit, and I actually use almost all of their implemented features, so no bloat at all.
  • Pacman and the AUR kick ass. They suit my needs, and as long as I verify my shit from the AUR, I will face no security issues.
  • I get turned on by DIY stuff, so you can see why I initially was drawn onto Arch.
  • Because who the fuck would want to compile each fucking package (Fucking Gentoo)

Why DWM?

  • I am pretty sure the same things for the distro applies here. The patches approach is better than just getting some bloated shit full of things I will never use.

    Side Note: I used DWM mainly cause it was written in C, and I wanted to strengthen my skills in it then by tinkering through the windows manager. I may change it in case I wanted to learn Lua, for instance.

Why only FOSS?

  • Are you really fucking asking why? [catches breath]

    You need to imagine with me for a couple of moments a world where a law was stated with the dawn of the internet that all the software shall be FOSS, or at least OSS. We will never find ourselves in cases where some shady companies as Fakebook or WhatsDap master in tracking users. Let's get back to our shitty world. Why would a person feel safe using some already-written code which he can not really check? How can anyone let an organization control his data for their sake without having the word in it? Monopolies arose basing on proprietary shit. Without laws that enforce open source applications, companies will monopolize, and our rights will be stripped. People say we are too late, but humans will be forced to apply such laws in the future as creativity ceases to exist. Every innovation is being hidden for the sake of profit.
