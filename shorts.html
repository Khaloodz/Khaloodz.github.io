Happy Birthday! | 11 Jun. 2021

"Today is my birthday. Or precisely, today is 11/06/21 which is before my birthday by about sixteen days. Sixteen is a perfect square. Perfect Squares are cool.
Back to our point, Let's assume I'm writing a letter for my birthday.
Hello Tokhy! Hope you die today. Bye!"
The above started as a long letter that I decided to write myself another tragic letter. But man, the whole miserable letter thing I intended to do and shit is pretty pathetic. Thus, I decided to write my future self a real birthday card. I hoped him to receive his most wanted present; uncontrollable and sudden death. Heart attacks are my and his favorite. Happy birthday!
