Introduction/What the Floof are cognitive functions?

Halloo everybody! Today I will be talking about cognitive functions and trying to explain them the best I can. Hopefully this is a helpful and easy to understand guide, and have you feeling like a pro in no time!

The Basics

Cognitive functions are basically what you use to perceive the world and make decisions. Think about it like an extension of the way the four letters of the MBT tell you how you think. However, cognitive functions give a little more detail than the four letters, as they show how they combine with the other letters to make a particular thinking pattern.
